About Nike

Introduction

Nike, Inc. is a global leader in the design, development, manufacturing, and marketing of athletic footwear, apparel, equipment, and accessories. Founded on January 25, 1964, by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike has become one of the most recognized and valuable sports brands worldwide.

Mission Statement

Nike's mission is to "bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world." The brand believes that if you have a body, you are an athlete.

Iconic Products

Nike is known for its innovative products, including:

  • The Air Max series
  • The Nike Air Jordan line
  • The Flyknit technology
  • Dri-FIT performance apparel

Global Presence

Nike operates in over 190 countries and employs tens of thousands of people worldwide. Its headquarters is located in Beaverton, Oregon, USA.

Sustainability

Nike is committed to sustainability and reducing its environmental impact through initiatives such as the Move to Zero campaign, which aims to achieve zero carbon and zero waste.

Learn More

To learn more about Nike, visit their official website: www.nike.com.
